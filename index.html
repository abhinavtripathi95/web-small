SHOOTING CLUB


IIT Kanpur is one of the very few institutes (might even be the only one) in India to encourage and facilitate the game of shooting.


Shooting Club is a body under Games and Sports Council, Students' Gymkhana, IIT Kanpur which provides a platform to the students to pursue their interest in shooting sports, mainly 10m Air Rifle and 10m Air Pistol category. Started originally as a hobby group in March 2013, it was converted into a recognised club on March 10, 2015. The efforts of Prachi Mishra are highly appreciated in this regard. She is considered to have given birth to the culture of shooting sports in IIT Kanpur.

Our Aim


MISSION: Shooting Club aims at creating competitive state and national level players. Every year a number of students from IITK participate in district, state and national level competitions.


VISION: There are plenty of problems faced by the club. The most important one is the location of the shooting range. The only shooting range in Kanpur is about 5 km away from the campus. We are making every effort possible in order to establish a classy shooting range inside the campus.
